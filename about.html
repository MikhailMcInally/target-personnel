Target Personnel

Target Personnel provides a wide range of staffing solutions to help both employers and employees find the perfect placement, right down to the smallest details. We go above and beyond expectations, providing expertly selected employees and covering all payroll costs for temporary placement including Workers Compensation, Canada pension, Employment Insurance, and Liability Insurance.
